Naomi Townsend

motherwrites

Naomi Townsend has been writing since she could hold a pencil. While she has come a long way from her first poem The Wizard stuck in a Blizzard at age 5, her process has not changed. Many of her poems are written in pencil on the back of napkins or haphazardly typed into the notes section of her phone. She lives in a small town in Tennessee with her partner and their son.
